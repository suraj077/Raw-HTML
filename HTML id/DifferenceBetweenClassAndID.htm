The Sentence

Kinds of Sentences.

Sentences are of four kinds:-

1.Those which make statement or assertions.

Humpty Dumpty sat on a wall

2.Those which ask Questions.

Where do you live ?

3.Those which express commands,requests, or entreaties

Be quite. Have mercy upon us

4.Those which express strong feelings.

How cold the night is ! What a shame !

Important Note

A sentence that make a statement or assertion is called a Declarative or Assertive sentence.

A sentence that ask a question is called an Interrogative sentance.

A sentence that express a command is called or an entreaty is called an Imperative sentence.

A sentence that expresses strong feeling is called an Exclamatory sentence.
